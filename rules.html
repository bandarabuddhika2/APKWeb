- The competition will be a team event under two categories, School Category and Open Category. - A maximum number of 4 teams (with 4 members each) can participate under School Category. - There is no restriction for the number of teams that can participate from Universities/Schools for the Open Category. - The participants for the school category must be under age 20 by 31st December 2020 and should be from the same school and from Sri Lanka. - All the teams (School/University) which are not from Sri Lanka should register under Open Category. (No age restrictions) - Team participants for the open category should either be from the same school (representing the old astronomical society of the school) or from the same university (representing the Astronomical Society of the university). Participants must be above age 20 by 31st December 2020. (The age restriction is only for Sri Lankan Teams) - Team participants from universities should be current undergraduates of the university. - The quiz will consist of 4 papers from the subjects Astrophysics, Cosmology, Observation, and Rocketry. - The papers will be delivered through our official online platform and the answers must also be submitted through the same platform. We will provide more information regarding the quiz to the registered teams. - The quiz will be held from 00 00 h to 03 00 h (+5 30h GMT) on 1stof June 2020 as a midnight quiz competition respecting the traditional practice. - We will announce the list of competitors after checking all your information provided in the registration form and will inform further steps to the contact numbers you mention. The organizing committee has the authority to reject any registration with doubtful information. - Please mention only the name of the School/University when you register several teams. The teams from the same school/University will be numbered (I-IV and so on) according to the registration time. Any team after the 4th registration will be rejected in the School Category. - The deadline for the registration is the 27th of May 2020. - A test quiz will be conducted on the 25th of May to help you familiarize yourself with our quiz platform, only for the teams registered prior to that date. - Winners will be presented with the AUDAX Zodiac Shield and valuable prices. Please provide accurate and active contact details when you fill the registration form. For any inquiry, send an email to astro.mcas@gmail.com
